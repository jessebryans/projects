What are CSS Blends Modes?

Blend Modes originally only in software like photoshop gave you the ability to take two pixels laid on top of each other and combine in different ways.

the property describes how an element's content should blend with the content of the element's direct parent and the element's background.

So basically what that means is that css blend modes gives you the ability to really play with and manipulate the way colors are displayed on your content

Multiply

multiplies the base pixel by blend color

Screen

Multiplies the inverse of the two pixel colors

Overlay

Depends on the base color. Lights get lighter or darks get darker

Darken

Darkens the image

Lighten

Ligtens the image
